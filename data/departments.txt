## Masvingo City Council Departments

Masvingo City Council operates through specialized departments.

## Health Department

- Runs council clinics and maternity wards
- Implements Impilo Electronic Health Record (EHR)
- Issues secure Health IDs

## Engineering Department

- Manages water, sewerage, roads, and infrastructure
- Handles inspections for licenses and building approvals

## Finance Department

- Oversees billing, payments, and budgeting
- Manages revenue collection and financial reporting

## Housing & Environmental Services

- Enforces sanitation and environmental by-laws
- Coordinates waste management and anti-litter campaigns
