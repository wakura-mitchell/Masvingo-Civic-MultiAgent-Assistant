## Bill Payments – Masvingo City Council

Residents can pay bills for water, rates, and licenses using online platforms or in-person methods.

## Online Payment Platforms

- Paynow
  - Accepts EcoCash, OneMoney, Telecash, Visa, Mastercard, Zimswitch cards.
  - Used for water bills, rates, and license renewals.

- Zikicash
  - Search for “City of Masvingo” under billers.
  - Accepts Zimswitch-enabled bank cards.

- Promun Portal
  - Offers balance enquiries, payment history, and SMS/email confirmations.

## In-Person Payments

- Civic Centre Treasury Office
- Local clinics (for license payments)
- Designated council payment points

## Notes

- SMS or email confirmation serves as proof of payment.
- Foreign residents can pay using Mastercard or Visa.
- For issues, contact the Revenue Hall or ward office.
