## Masvingo City Council By-laws

Masvingo City Council enforces municipal by-laws to maintain public order, health, and infrastructure integrity.

## Key By-laws

- Hawkers and Street Vendors By-law (2022)
  - Regulates vending zones and licensing.
  - Prohibits vending in undesignated areas.
  - Offenders may be fined or have goods confiscated.

- Advertising By-law (2022)
  - Requires approval for billboards, posters, and signage.
  - Unauthorized advertising is subject to removal and fines.

- Traffic and Clamping By-law (2015, amended 2022)
  - Governs prepaid parking discs and clamping procedures.
  - Vehicles parked illegally may be clamped or towed.

- Environmental Conservation By-law (2023)
  - Protects wetlands, indigenous plants, and wildlife.
  - Regulates brick making and forest product harvesting.

- Food Hygiene By-law (2022)
  - Sets standards for food preparation and sanitation.
  - Applies to restaurants, lodges, and street vendors.

- Hairdressers and Barbershop By-law (2022)
  - Requires licensing and adherence to cleanliness standards.

- Anti-litter and Human Excreta By-laws (2022)
  - Enforces public cleanliness and sanitation.
