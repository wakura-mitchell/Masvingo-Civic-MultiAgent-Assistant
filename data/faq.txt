### How do I pay my water bill?
Use Paynow, Zikicash, or Promun Portal. You can also pay at the Civic Centre.

### Where do I apply for a shop license?
Visit your local clinic and speak to a Health Inspector.

### Who is my ward councillor?
Refer to the council_contacts.txt file or visit the Civic Centre.

### What are the water supply hours?
Most residents receive water for ~3 hours/day due to infrastructure challenges.

### Can I pay bills from outside Zimbabwe?
Yes, using Mastercard or Visa via Paynow.
